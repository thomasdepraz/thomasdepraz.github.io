Habemus Papam

Habemus Papam is a boardgame about cardinals trying to become the next pope during the 1492's Conclave. It's designed for 4 players each of them taking the place of Rodriguo Borgia, Asciano Sforza, Oliviero Carafa or Giuliano della Rovere.
Gather influence, lie to your opponents, stab them in the back, corrupt cardinals, every means are great when yo want to become the Pope in 1492.

It was created over the course of one school year by 3 game designers, including myself, and 3 game artists. In our team, everyone had a specific task. For example, I was charged of prototyping, so i had to make a new prototype everytime there was a major update on the game's graphics.
I am very proud of this project and what we achieved in one year.
